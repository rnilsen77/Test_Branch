My UX Skills

Wireframing

I have developed and perfected the ability to create Wireframes for my designs. These wireframes are used to iterate upon an idea as well as conduct users tests.

User Research

One major skill that I've developed is the ability to conduct User Research. It is a vital piece of the UX design process and allows me to create the best product possible for the needs of the user.

Interaction Design

Interaction Design is the ability to design the ways users interact and flow through a product. This skill is vital to the UX Design process.
